gcc -o filename filename.c /your/path/to/static/libopenblas.a -lpthread

On my machine:
/usr/lib/libopenblas.a


how to change threads number:
export OPENBLAS_NUM_THREADS=4 (depends on your machine)

Compiling with static library will create a big binary file. OpenBLAS can also
be dynamically link by compiling your code with -lopenblas option.

gcc -I /dir/of/your/libopenblas.so/file -o your_binary_file your_code_file.c -lpthread -lopenblas
